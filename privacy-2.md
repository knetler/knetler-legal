# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 30, 2026**

Your privacy matters to Knetler. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. What We Collect

When you interact with Knetler, we may collect:

- **Email address** — when you sign up for our email list or purchase a product
- **Name** — provided during checkout or sign-up
- **Payment information** — processed securely by our payment provider (we do not store card details)
- **Usage data** — pages visited, time on site, and general location (via analytics tools)

---

## 2. How We Collect It

We collect data through:

- **Lovable** — our website platform, which handles forms and user data collection
- **Email sign-up forms** — for our newsletter and free resources
- **Checkout** — when you purchase a product or service

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

- Deliver products, courses, and resources you've purchased
- Send emails you've opted into (tips, tutorials, offers)
- Improve our website and content
- Respond to support requests

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 4. Email Communications

By joining our email list, you agree to receive emails from Knetler. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party tools that may process your data:

- **Lovable** — website and form platform
- **Payment processors** — for handling transactions securely
- **Email platform** — for sending newsletters and product delivery

Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as you are a subscriber or customer. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Opt out of marketing emails at any time

---

## 8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics and functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
